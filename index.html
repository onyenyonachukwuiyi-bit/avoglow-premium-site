Meet Team Avoglow.

The student team supporting Avoglow—Atari Farms’ flagship circular innovation brand—where surplus avocado becomes premium natural wellness and cosmetic products.

The challenge

Good food should not go to waste.

Smallholder farmers face unreliable markets, low prices and significant post-harvest losses. We help turn underutilised produce into opportunity.

Our vision

Resilient, inclusive value chains.

To build agricultural value chains where smallholder farmers thrive through sustainable market access, value addition and circular innovation.

Our mission

Value that reaches further.

To create sustainable agricultural opportunities through circular value addition, innovation, market access and inclusive partnerships.
